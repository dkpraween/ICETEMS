ICETEMS-2023


Call for Papers

Original unpublished contributions are solicited for presentation at the 1st International Conference on on Emerging Technologies in Engineering and Management Sciences (ICETEMS-2023). Papers are not allowed to be submitted in parallel to any other conference or journal.

Original papers are invited on the subject areas including, but not limited to the following:

Track-1: Electronics and Communications Engineering

  • Communications circuits and systems
  • Intelligent Automation in Communication System
  • 5G/6G Technologies
  • Vehicular Networks and Communication
  • Cognitive Radio and AI-Enabled Networks
  • Analog, Digital, RF & Mixed Signal Circuits & Systems
  • Image Processing and Multimedia, Biomedical Imaging
  • Smart industrial IoT
  • Robotics for defence applications
  • Drones and Drone Applications
  • IoT applications to Agriculture and Smart health monitoring systems

Track-2: Electrical and Electronics Engineering

  • Smart Grid Technologies
  • Micro-grids and Distributed Generation
  • Power System Automation, Protection and Relaying
  • Intelligent Instrumentation, Control and Robotics
  • Power System Restructuring and Deregulation
  • Power Semiconductor Controlled Electric Drives
  • High Voltage Engineering and Insulation Materials
  • Nano Devices and Integrated Systems
  • Power Electronics
  • Power Systems

Track-3:Applied Computing & Big Data

  • Image processing, Pattern recognition, Big data
  • Data mining, Artificial intelligence
  • Computer vision, Computer graphics, Cloud computing
  • Distributed computing, Bioinformatics, Semantic web
  • Software engineering, Web technologies
  • E-Learning and E-Governance, Data/Web mining and Knowledge Discovery
  • Data and Information Quality Control
  • Cluster Computing, Supercomputing, Cloud Computing
  • Autonomic Computing, P2P Computing
  • Mobile Computing, Grid and Semantic Grid Computing
  • Computer and Network Architecture, Network Communication
  • Network Operating system, Distributed Networks and systems
  • Reconfigurable Computing Systems, Event Driven Computing
  • Natural Language Processing
  • Internet of Things
  • Quantum Computing

Track-4:Civil Engineering

  • Green Materials and Technology
  • 3D Printing Technology
  • Earthquake and Wind Engineering
  • Structural Analysis and Design

  • Sustainability in Construction Management

  • Sustainability in Water Resource Management and Environmental Engineering

  • Remote sensing and GIS Applications in Civil Engineering

Track-5:Mechanical Engineering

  • Advancement in Renewable Energy Sources
  • Novel materials for thin film solar cells

  • Computing in Applied Mechanics and Product Design
